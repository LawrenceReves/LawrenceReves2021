I’m Lawrence, a graphic designer based in Winnipeg, Manitoba. I am currently in the second year of the Graphic Design program at Red River College. My approach when designing is to always be uncomplicated, inclusive, and effective. With being immersed in modern design from a young age, I tend to pull inspiration from art, shapes, and colour.

Red River Exibhition

Identity + Campaign + Web

Rebrand of Red River Exhibition 2021. The goal of this rebrand was to create new artwork and theme graphics that could be implemented into the following expressions. Through use of contrasting primary colours, and graphic simplification used for the Ferris wheel and sunset — a desired sense of summer, warmth, movement and nostalgia is created. The simple yet bold theme graphics stand to create a stamp that aim to become synonymous with the exhibition and allow the option for similar graphics to be created in cohesion with the existing shapes shown. Typography takes the form in a font somewhat unconventional in hopes to create an identity that feels so incredibly different from its predecessor that the audience can’t help but partake in the Red River Exhibition.
