Manitoba Studio Fund 2021

Web + Identity

I was Lucky enough to design the Manitoba Studio Fund 2021 website during my second work placement this year with the amazing people at Manoverboard. I also got to collaborate with a DMD student who's responsibilty was to code the website. They allowed me to really make the project my own while still adhering to brand guidelines. My primary objective for this website design was to create something that at its core felt warm, colourful, and unique — a few things in a list of what I think about when I think of black, indigenous, and people of colour. Designating each section of the site a neutral/’flesh-like’ tone from the brand guide was the foremost way in which I chose to achieve that. Photos were treated monochromatically to allow the page colour to remain a unified focal point. Fivo Sans Modern — a strong sans serif with personality for headers is accompanied by Pragmatica from the brand guide for optimal readability. For movement/interactivity, I wanted to add a subtle layer of dynamics to the site while not distracting from the content. Overall, I felt incredibly lucky to be a part of this project, and I thank the GDC, and all the of the scholarship founds + founders for helping BIPOC designers by forming The Manitoba Studio Fund.
