Sex Workers of Winnipeg Action Coalition (SWWAC)

Identity + Campaign

Rebrand of Sex Workers of Winnipeg Action Coalition. SWWAC’s goal is to make things safer for sex workers. Their vision is to see sex work decriminalized and destigmatized, for everyone to have autonomy over the work they do and their working conditions, and that their group be a space where people learn, grow, and share. The rebrand aims to create an identity that focuses on the humanization of the individuals in this line of work. The logo created showcases the beauty of the people who are in the industry — with inspiration from figure drawings and sculptures. Overall, the rebrand’s objective is to construct visuals and content that educates individuals on what they don’t know about sex work. Through typography, colour choice, and photography — a sense of appropriacy is spotlighted for the collective goal of destigmatization.
